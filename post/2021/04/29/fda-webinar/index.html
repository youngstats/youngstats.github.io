Recent Advances in Functional Data Analysis

The fourth “One World webinar” organized by YoungStatS will take place on June 20th, 2021. The topic of this webinar is on Functional Data Analysis. Selected young European researchers active in this area of research will present their contributions on spherical functional autoregressions, additive models, and clustering methods for functional data, with the focus on both theoretical developments and applications.

When & Where:

  • Wednesday, June 30th, 16:30 CEST

  • Online, via Zoom. The registration form will be provided on the YoungStatS website. Further details and the Zoom link will be sent to the registered addresses only.

Speakers:

  • Alessia Caponera (École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland): “Asymptotics for spherical functional autoregressions”

  • Alexander Volkmann (Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin, Germany): “Multivariate Functional Additive Mixed Models”

  • Fabio Centofanti (University of Naples Federico II, Italy): “Sparse and Smooth Functional Data Clustering”

Discussant:

  • Juhyun Park (École nationale supérieure d'informatique pour l'industrie et l'entreprise, France)

The webinar is part of YoungStatS project of the Young Statisticians Europe initiative (FENStatS) supported by the Bernoulli Society for Mathematical Statistics and Probability.

For more information, please visit our website.
