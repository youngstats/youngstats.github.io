Online Seminars

One World YoungStatS webinar

We are delighted to announce the creation of the One World YoungStatS webinar Series! Our goal in creating this seminar series is to provide a platform for our community to continue interacting and growing in spite of the current health crisis. The Online Seminar Series will accompany the YoungStatS blog and provide insight into best and most recent work done in all areas of statistics

Seminars will be held on Zoom on Wednesday at 4:00 pm CET (10:00 am ET / 9:00 am CT / 7:00 am PT). Each session will feature one or two talks by invited young research scholars, followed by a 15 minutes discussion led by an invited discussant.
